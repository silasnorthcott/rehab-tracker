About This Project

The Rehab Adherence & Reflection Tracker helps patients log rehab sessions and reflect on their progress in a structured, professional format.

Users can:

  • Log exercises, effort, pain, and adherence
  • Track weekly and monthly averages
  • Reflect on recovery progress
  • Export data for review by a physiotherapist

Built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to demonstrate patient-centered rehabilitation tools.
